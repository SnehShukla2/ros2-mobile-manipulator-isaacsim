How to read this chart: The arm base (shoulder joint) sits at the origin (0, 0). X = forward distance from the base; Z = height above the base. The shaded zone shows all wrist positions reachable by varying elbow and wrist angles. Shoulder rotation sweeps this entire zone through ±180° around the vertical axis, creating a 3D reachable volume. The zone is asymmetric because the elbow only bends forward (0–150°), not backward.
